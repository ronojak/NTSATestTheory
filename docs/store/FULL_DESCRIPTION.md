DriveTheory CBT helps you prepare for driving theory exams with timed, randomized multiple-choice tests. Practice entirely offline with a bundled question set, see instant scores, and review your history over time.

Key features
- Randomized questions and answer options
- Timed exams with autosave during session
- Instant scoring with simple results view
- Attempt history to track progress
- Works fully offline; no account needed

This is an initial release focused on reliability and speed on a wide range of Android devices.
